RSWhy. Your RSI management system

RSIs are hard to manage. They require physiotherapy & daily treatment, which are limiting and aggravating.

That's why we made RSWhy. With just a small daily session for analysis and massage,
you can keep track of your ailment before it hurts to do the things you love.

Our Methedology

With 3 simple steps every day, you won't have to worry for your RSI any longer.

Calibration

First we calibrate your arm and wrist with our system using a signature machine learning algorithm to refine the inputs from the Myo armband.

Massage & Analysis

Now you're able to enjoy a calm session where our vibration cuff will massage out the tensions in your muscles, as well as collect EMG (Electromyography) data to assess the health of your motor neurons.

Save Your Data

By storing your data online, you'll be able to check your daily performance metrics over time; giving you a larger picture of how your RSI is progressing.
